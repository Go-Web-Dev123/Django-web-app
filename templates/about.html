Paperless hospitals is a concept in which we are reducing the usage of papers in hospitals by digitalizing them. Digitalization is done by registering the patients using their unique Mobile Number for each patients in which the information will be stored in a unique way in the Database. We will store all the information like Xray , reports, lab test results and patient details in the Database and it will be secured by our IT professionals, and so it will be used for retrieving all kind of data of that particular patient for that patient. This may serve Digitalization and reduce the use of huge amount of papers in Hospital.

So let's make Hospitals go Paperless and move Future with the Technology.
